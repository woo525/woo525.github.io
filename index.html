I am a graduate researcher at POSTECH AI Graduate School. My research interests include computational photography, image restoration, burst imaging, ISP-aware image synthesis, and camera-aware low-level vision.

I am particularly interested in building physically grounded data synthesis pipelines and robust restoration models that generalize across devices, cameras, and imaging conditions.

My recent work focuses on burst imaging, deblurring, defocus simulation, cross-device generalization, and camera ISP modeling.
